Chocolate Ice Cubes

Description

Easy recipe for dessert and chocolate lovers out there who want to spend minimal time and hassle free cooking involved.

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tsbp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Bring milk, water, cocoa powder, and sugar to a simmer in a saucepan, whisking until the limps dissolve.
  2. Remove from heat, add chocolate chips and stir until the chocolate melts.
  3. Let it cool for 5 minutes, then pour into an ice cube tray and freeze.
  4. Once frozen enjoy to your hearts content.
